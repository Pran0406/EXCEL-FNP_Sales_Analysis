# 🎁 Ferns & Petals Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview  
Developed an interactive sales analysis dashboard for **Ferns & Petals (FNP)** to evaluate business performance across revenue, customer behavior, product demand, and seasonal trends.  
The project transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights to improve sales strategy and customer satisfaction.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement  

Analyze FNP sales data to uncover insights related to:
- Revenue performance  
- Customer purchasing behavior  
- Product demand across occasions  
- Delivery efficiency  

The goal is to support **data-driven decisions** for optimizing sales and enhancing customer experience.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description  

The dataset includes:
- 🛍 Product details  
- 📦 Order information  
- 👤 Customer data  
- 📅 Order & delivery dates  
- 🎉 Occasions (Diwali, Valentine’s Day, Birthdays, etc.)  

---

## 🔄 Data Processing  

- 🧹 Performed data cleaning by removing duplicates, handling missing values, and standardizing formats  
- 🔧 Applied Excel functions (IF, XLOOKUP, SUMIFS, COUNTIFS) for data transformation and analysis  
- 📊 Built Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts to summarize key metrics  
- 🎯 Used slicers and filters to create interactive dashboard views  
- 📈 Calculated business metrics such as Revenue, AOV, and Order-to-Delivery Time  

---

## 📈 Key Insights  

### 💰 Revenue Analysis  
- Identified overall revenue trends and peak sales periods across the year  
- Certain occasions contribute a significant share of total revenue  

### 📦 Delivery Performance  
- Average order-to-delivery time analyzed to evaluate operational efficiency  
- Observed delays during high-demand periods (festivals/occasions)  

### 📅 Monthly Sales Trends  
- Sales show clear seasonal fluctuations, with spikes during major gifting occasions  
- Off-season months show relatively lower performance  

### 🏆 Top Products  
- Top-performing products contribute a major portion of total revenue  
- Indicates strong product preference concentration  

### 👤 Customer Spending Behavior  
- Average customer spending (AOV) highlights purchasing patterns  
- Helps identify high-value customers  

### 📊 Top 5 Product Performance  
- A small group of products consistently drives sales performance  
- Useful for inventory and marketing prioritization  

### 🌆 City-wise Orders  
- Top 10 cities contribute a large share of total orders  
- Indicates geographic concentration of demand  

### ⚖️ Order Quantity vs Delivery Time  
- Higher order quantities slightly increase delivery time  
- Suggests need for logistics optimization during bulk orders  

### 🎉 Occasion-wise Revenue  
- Occasions like **Diwali, Valentine’s Day, and Birthdays** generate peak revenue  
- Seasonal demand plays a major role in business performance  

### 🎁 Product Popularity by Occasion  
- Specific products perform better during specific occasions  
- Enables targeted marketing and product bundling strategies
